Premium Reserve Rosé

Piattelli's Premium Reserve Rosé of Malbec showcases the efforts of both our Mendoza winery as well as our Cafayate winery. We use the grapes from our Grand Reserve Malbec in Mendoza and combine them with the Torrontés grapes that are grown in their native home of Cafayate to create a wine that is refreshing and light, and truly brings together the best of both terroirs.

AGING.

Two months of aging to preserve its citrus dimension.

Notas de Cata.

El Premium Reserve Rosé es muy aromaticó, intenso, de hermoso color rosa brillante. Es un vino que conquista los sentidos por sus aromas florales aportados por la presencia del Torrontés. En boca es muy fresco, de textura sedosa y cuerpa medio. Se perciben, además, delicadas notas de cereza y frutilla. Su final es equilibrado y persistente.

PAIRINGS.

Chicken, sausage, mild asian cuisine, sushi, fried vegetables, seafood.
