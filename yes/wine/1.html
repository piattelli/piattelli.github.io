AGING.

The Grand Reserve Malbec grapes were aged 13 months in French and American Oak barrels.

TASTING NOTES.

A rich and fragrant wine with a deep purple hue, Grand Reserve Malbec envelops the senses with an exquisite bouquet of sweet and spicy plum, blossoms in the glass to reveal notes of tea leaves and cassis, lingers on the palate with a moderate tannin pull, and finishes with understated-yet-resonant oak.
