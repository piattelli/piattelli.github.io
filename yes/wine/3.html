RESERVE CABERNET SAUVIGNON.

High in the Andes surrounding Salta, the distinctively colorful northwest Argentina mountains formed by many geological layers - inspired our labels. The splendor of this area, with it's pure water, high altitude and dramatic temperature swings, makes this not only one of the most beautiful but also one of the premier grape growing regions in the world.

AGING.

Aged for 10 months in French and American Oak barrels and bottled-aged an additional 6 months.

TASTING NOTES.

Deep purple in color with bright aromas of red fruits and spices. A full-bodied and mouth-filling red with smooth tannins, leading to a long and elegant finish.

PAIRINGS.

Red meats and roasted vegetables.
