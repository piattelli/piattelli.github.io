PREMIUM RESERVE MALBEC.

High in the Andes surrounding Salta, the distinctly colorful northwest Argentina mountains formed by many geological layers - inspired our labels. The splendor of this area, with its pure water, high altitude and dramatic temperature swings, makes this not only one of the most beautiful but also one of the premier grape growing regions in the world.

AGING.

Aged for 8 months in French and American Oak barrels and then bottle-aged an additional 6 months.

TASTING NOTES.

Purple in color with aromatic notes of ripe plum and violet, Reserve Malbec's fruity bouquet delights the senses. The mouth is marked by structure and softness. Full-bodied and bright, this is a very food.

PAIRINGS.

Robust dishes with rich flavors and red meats.
