Grand Reserve Malbec

High in the Salta Andes, the phenomenal beauty of the seven colors typical of the northern Argentine mountains, inspired our new labels. The splendor of this area, with its pure water, high altitude and vast temperature variations, make this not only a beautiful region, but also a region where the best grapes in the world are grown. Hopefully someday, you have the pleasure of visiting this charming valley, but in the meantime we invite you to taste one of our award-winning wines that reflects this beautiful region.

Aging.

12 months in French and American oak barrels.

Tasting Notes.

This wine represents the best of the crops. An intense red color and aromas of black fruits, vanilla and smoke. These aromas are transformed to give the wine great structure and body on the palate. A soft fruit-forward flavor and finished strong and balanced.

Parings.

Pork, lamb, red meats and spicy, grilled vegetables.
